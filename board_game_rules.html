Game Overview

👥 2-4 Players ⏳ 45-60 Mins 🎂 Ages 10+

Welcome to Galactic Harvest, where interstellar corporations vie for dominance over uncharted fertile sectors of space. Plant plasma crops, optimize cosmic irrigation channels, and harvest stellar energy before the supernova ruins the sector!

💡 Core Concept: The game ends immediately when the Solar Countdown die reaches zero. Make every single action count!

Components

Before beginning your setup, check that your copy contains the following elements:

1x Sector Board

The main modular star cluster array.

40x Plasma Seed Cubes

10 distinct neon tokens per player color.

24x Action Cards

Standard deck used to draft seasonal tactics.

1x Solar Countdown Die

Custom 6-sided round pacing marker.

Setup Guide

Follow these quick setup instructions to kick off your match:

  1. Place the modular Sector Board in the center of the table.
  2. Each player chooses a faction color and takes all matching Plasma Cubes.
  3. Shuffle the Action Cards and deal 3 cards face-down to each player.
  4. Place the Solar Countdown Die on the sector marked '6'.

How to Play

The game is played over a series of rounds. Each round consists of three fluid phases:

1. The Planning Phase

Players simultaneously select one Action Card from their hand and place it face down in front of them.

2. The Deployment Phase

In turn order, players flip their card and execute the primary actions (e.g., Cultivate, Pollinate, or Harvest).

3. Cosmic Cleanup Phase

Pass your remaining hand of cards to the left, and decrement the Solar Countdown Die by 1 tick.

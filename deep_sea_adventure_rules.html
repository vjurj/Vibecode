How to Play

The game is played over 3 rounds

At the beginning of each player's turn, reduce air by the amount of chips they hold. Even if the counter reaches 0, the player is allowed to finish their turn. The players that are already back at the submarine are not affected.

Before rolling, you must declare the direction you are moving towards (the sea or the submarine)

You may only declare once per round if you go back towards the submarine. You cannot revert this choice until the next round.

When moving, subtract the number of chips from the dice roll. For example, a roll of 4 with 2 chips means that you move 2 spaces (4 rolled - 2 chips = 2)

Each player that has reached the submarine will score points for all of their chips

The players that have not reached the submarine will lose all of their chips. Those will be placed next to the chip furthest away from the submarine, in the next empty location. Discard them as stacks of 3 chips.

The discarded chip stacks count as 1 chip per stack for the purposes of decreasing air and spaces moved

At the end of each round, all empty chips will be discarded

The player farthest from the submarine will start the next round
